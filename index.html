About Me

Engineering Excellence Through Precision

Motivated detail-driven structural engineer with a passion for precise steel detailing and smooth project delivery.

With a Master's degree in Civil Engineering from the University of Architecture, Civil Engineering and Geodesy (UACEG) in Sofia, Bulgaria, I bring a strong foundation in practical problem-solving and technical planning to every project.

My UK NARIC/ENIC certification confirms my qualification at RQF Level 7, reflecting an advanced postgraduate level of study that meets international standards.

I am friendly, reliable, and always aiming to create work that is safe, clear, and easy for others to use. As the founder of M I V LTD, I now lead projects from concept to completion.
